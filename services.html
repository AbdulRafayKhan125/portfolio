Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes! I offer affordable hosting solutions for all my clients. Instead of paying for expensive hosting elsewhere, you can host your website with me for a small fee. This includes regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support to ensure your website runs smoothly at all times.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I create are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. This ensures your visitors have a great experience regardless of the device they're using.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly. These packages include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, and credit cards. For larger projects, I typically require a 50% deposit upfront, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

Can you help with content creation?

While I primarily focus on design and development, I can provide basic content guidance and structure. For comprehensive content creation, I can recommend professional copywriters who specialize in web content.
